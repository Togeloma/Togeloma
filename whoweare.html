Members


Victor Zhao - Founder of Togeloma

Greetings, I'm Victor Zhao, a dedicated Canadian high school student with a profound passion for addressing the often overlooked issue of childhood cancer. In pursuit of making a meaningful impact, I've co-founded "Togeloma" alongside other enthusiastic teens in Vancouver. As the founder of this organization, I am determined that by taking small steps toward raising awareness and support for children battling cancer, we can make a great impact on the community that we are in.



Vincent Guo - IT technician

Vincent is a grade 10 high school student in Canada who is passionate about data science and engineering. He is dedicated to help spread awareness in support of those fighting cancer. He is a member in "Togeloma" to help create a meaningful impact. Outside of this project, he enjoys playing golf and basketball.



Joanna Luan - Media Manager

Hi! My name is Joanna Luan who is currently in Grade 11 at Sentinel Secondary School. I am the Social Media Manager for Togeloma and have been working with Victor for a very long time on this project. Being involved in this organization has shaped my character into someone who is a strong academic achiever as well as a hard-working individual. I’ve not only had the ability to balance my school work with the work from the organization but also gained a better understanding of what we can do to help the cancer community around us.



Kelly Yu - Designer

Hello, I'm Kelly Yu, a 16-year-old from Vancouver known for art and fashion achievements. I won 1st place in the “11th Youth World Cup Live Painting Competition” and walked runways at Vancouver Fashion Week. Beyond modeling, I founded "Azure," blending art and fashion. As a "Togeloma" designer, I'm dedicated to addressing childhood cancer and raising awareness and support.
