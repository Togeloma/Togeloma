Victor Zhao

Founder of Togeloma

Greetings, I'm Victor Zhao, a dedicated Canadian high school student with a profound passion for addressing the often overlooked issue of childhood cancer. In pursuit of making a meaningful impact, I've co-founded "Togeloma" alongside other enthusiastic teens in Vancouver. As the founder of this organization, I am determined that by taking small steps toward raising awareness and support for children battling cancer, we can make a great impact on the community that we are in.
